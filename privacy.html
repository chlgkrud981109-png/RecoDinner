Privacy Policy

At RecoDinner, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

RecoDinner is a client-side application. All the memos you create are stored locally in your browser's local storage. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data or the content of your memos.

How We Use Your Data

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use your data in any way.
